### Phishing ###
Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
Question 1: Which of the following is a common tactic used in phishing attacks?

A) Sending emails with attachments that contain malware.
B) Impersonating a trusted organization to steal sensitive information.
C) Using fake social media accounts to promote products.
D) Offering free software downloads to unsuspecting users.

Answer: B) Impersonating a trusted organization to steal sensitive information.

Question 2: What is the primary goal of a phishing attack?

A) To steal your computer’s hardware.
B) To trick victims into providing sensitive personal or financial information.
C) To disable a victim’s device permanently.
D) To send fake social media messages to your contacts.

Answer: B) To trick victims into providing sensitive personal or financial information.

Question 3: How can you protect yourself from phishing attacks?

A) Always click on email links to verify messages.
B) Use multi-factor authentication (MFA) for your online accounts.
C) Disable your email account’s spam filters.
D) Open all unsolicited email attachments to check for malware.

Answer: B) Use multi-factor authentication (MFA) for your online accounts.
