### Creating Strong Passwords ###
Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
Question 1:
What is the minimum recommended length for a strong password?

A) 6 characters
B) 8 characters
C) 12 characters
D) 16 characters

Answer: C) 12 characters

Question 2:
Which of the following is a characteristic of a strong password?

A) It is based on a dictionary word like “password” or “letmein.”
B) It contains a combination of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.
C) It uses personal information like your birthdate or your pet’s name.
D) It is exactly 8 characters long and easy to remember.

Answer: B) It contains a combination of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

Question 3:
What is the purpose of using a password manager?

A) To store your passwords in a secure location and generate strong passwords.
B) To help you remember your passwords without using strong ones.
C) To automatically share your passwords with others for convenience.
D) To create a new password for every website you visit without any need for security.

Answer: A) To store your passwords in a secure location and generate strong passwords.
