### Phishing and Social Engineering ###
Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
Question 1: Which of the following is a sign of a phishing email?

A) The email contains a personal greeting, such as “Dear [Your Name].”
B) The email includes a request for urgent action, like “Your account will be suspended unless you act now!”
C) The email is from an official, well-known company website.
D) The email contains only relevant, personalized information without any errors.

Answer: B) The email includes a request for urgent action, like “Your account will be suspended unless you act now!”

Question 2: What is a common tactic used in social engineering attacks?

A) Offering secure encryption for files.
B) Manipulating individuals into divulging confidential information by exploiting trust.
C) Installing antivirus software without the user’s consent.
D) Providing legitimate technical support from a recognized provider.

Answer: B) Manipulating individuals into divulging confidential information by exploiting trust.

Question 3: Which of the following is the best way to protect yourself from phishing and social engineering attacks?

A) Ignore any suspicious emails or calls and delete them immediately.
B) Share passwords only when asked in official-looking emails.
C) Always verify the source before clicking on links or providing sensitive information.
D) Install software that blocks all incoming emails.

Answer: C) Always verify the source before clicking on links or providing sensitive information.
