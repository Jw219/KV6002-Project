### Man-in-the-Middle (MitM) Attacks ###
Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
Question 1: What is the primary goal of a Man-in-the-Middle (MitM) attack?

A) To infect a device with malware.
B) To intercept and alter communication between two parties.
C) To disrupt the availability of a website.
D) To spread viruses through email.

Answer: B) To intercept and alter communication between two parties.

Question 2: Which of the following can help protect against MitM attacks when using a website?

A) Use unencrypted HTTP instead of HTTPS.
B) Check for a padlock symbol and ensure the site uses HTTPS.
C) Disable your antivirus software to increase connection speed.
D) Share login credentials freely to avoid forgetting them.

Answer: B) Check for a padlock symbol and ensure the site uses HTTPS.

Question 3: Which of the following is a potential result of a Man-in-the-Middle attack?

A) A server overload due to too many requests.
B) Unauthorized access to private data or accounts.
C) Data being permanently lost from a device.
D) Unsolicited pop-up ads appearing on your device.

Answer: B) Unauthorized access to private data or accounts.
